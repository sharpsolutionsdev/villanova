Villanova

we put the fun in funk.

Villanova is a high-voltage, 3-piece funk rock powerhouse from the Northwest of England.

Fusing the rhythm of funk, the groove of disco, and the raw energy of classic rock, we bring an electrifying sound that's impossible to ignore. Our music is designed to move your feet, shake your soul, and keep you coming back for more.

Whether you're on the dancefloor or in the front row, we're here to bring the party, ignite the crowd, and remind you what live music is all about.

Meet the Band

Taylor

Bassist

"Taylor lays down the foundation of Villanova’s sound with deep, rhythmic basslines that bridge funk and alternative rock. Drawing inspiration from legends like Flea and Bernard Edwards, his style adds groove and drive to every track, bringing a unique energy to the band's live shows."

@krunckus

Sean

Guitarist/Vocalist

"Sean co-founded Villanova with Charlie in 2023, blending melodic guitar hooks with raw vocal power to shape the band’s sonic identity. Influenced by artists like John Frusciante and Dave Grohl, his songwriting balances introspection and intensity, creating music that resonates on and off stage."

@sean.89712

Charlie

Drummer

"After forming the band with Sean in 2023, Charlie has been the core of the groove within the band's funk/rock driven sound. With a mix of classic rock, funk and disco beats, he takes inspiration from drummers like Stewart Copeland and Jeff Porcaro along with bands ranging from Chic to The Foo Fighters."

@charlie_hollis_21
